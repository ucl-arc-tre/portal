As the Information Asset Administrator (Administrator) of a “Study” you work with delegated responsibility from the Information Asset Owner and are responsible for the day-to-day management of specific data assets. The purpose of this form is to gain your confirmation of this responsibility. A public copy of this agreement can be found [here](https://isms.arc.ucl.ac.uk/agreement_templates/rism19-Information_Asset_Administrator_agreement).

This agreement aligns with the [Documented Information](https://isms.arc.ucl.ac.uk/) for the Research Data Information Security Management System including:

- [Information Security Policy](https://isms.arc.ucl.ac.uk/rism07-research_data_isms_information_security_policy/)
- [Roles and Responsibilities](https://isms.arc.ucl.ac.uk/rism02-roles_and_responsibilities/)

## Agreement

I understand that as Information Asset Administrator, I must:

- be accountable to the Information Asset Owner,
- be responsible for any aspects of the management of confidential information assigned to me by the Information Asset Owner,
- undertake suitable information security training regularly,
- ensure that users I grant data access to undertake suitable information security training regularly,
- consult with the Information Asset Owner if/when my Information Asset Administrator responsibilities become unclear, and
- be an individual with a UCL staff employment contract.
